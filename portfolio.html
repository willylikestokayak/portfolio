WYD

WYM (What You Mean) utilizes the IBM Watson Tone Analyzer API to analyze tone of a user's submitted text. WYM is able to detect a range of 7 different tones and provide the user with tone feedback of the text that was provided. Tech used: Reactjs, Node.js, Mongo, Materialize. Collaborators: Justen Cracraft, Stephanie Hester, Ari Minton, Chad Coker.
